Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Find direct, human responses below.

Are my graphic files uploaded securely during compress/resize operations?

No files are ever uploaded! Toolifya was constructed on standard progressive client technologies. When you select or drag files into upload containers, javascript components stream their raw binary chunks into localized FileReader scopes. Every sizing, cropping, or conversion is processed inside your local browser memory using Canvas buffers.

What makes Toolifya completely free to use without daily limits?

We sustain this service without membership billing models by integration with premium programmatic visual banner advertisements (Google AdSense and Adsterra). This model protects your access from payment wall overlays or subscriptions while allowing us to continuously fund development and hosting services globally.

Can I compress or resize large quantities of items in bulk?

Yes! High-volume operations are fully supported on Toolifya. The Compressor, format converters, and WebP modules compile multiple selected images asynchronously, generating customized tables of completed actions. You can download items individually or as consolidated compressed archives in single zip collections.
