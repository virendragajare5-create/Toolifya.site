Frequently Asked Questions

Will my image lose quality during resize processes?

When scaling images down, bicubic interpolation ensures pixels regroup cleanly with zero perceptible fidelity loss. When magnifying images significantly above 150%, mild blur can present as browser engines must interpret new pixels, which we mitigate using optimal anti-aliasing configurations.

Does your resizer support transparent backgrounds?

Yes! If you upload a transparent PNG and select standard PNG or WebP as your target output format, all alpha levels and translucent parameters will be preserved cleanly. However, if you choose JPEG as the final format, transparent elements are backfilled with neutral white solid block colors.

Can I enter any specific custom pixel size constraints?

Absolutely. Our Pixel option accommodates custom heights and widths. If the Aspect Ratio lock constraint is checked, entering value modifications inside one field dynamically coordinates the appropriate proportional counterpart, avoiding stretched results.
