Frequently Asked Questions

Sizing toolkit answers.

Will resizing blur my original image?
Rescaling downward remains exceptionally sharp. Stretching graphics far past original width constraints will introduce native pixel interpolation artifacts.

Are transparency structures kept?
Yes, choosing PNG or WebP targets preserves absolute transparency channels. Exporting under JPEG outputs solid white backups instead.

Can I specify customized dimension values?
Yes. Enter any custom numbers into the pixel coordinate inputs and toggle Aspect Ratio rules off to resize entirely freely.
