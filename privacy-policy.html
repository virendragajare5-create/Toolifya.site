Privacy Policy & Cookie Consent

Effective Date: May 22, 2026 | Last Updated: May 22, 2026

1. Client-Side Image Isolation

At **Toolifya** (accessible at https://toolifya.site), user privacy is our absolute priority. We do not use remote databases or cloud processors to analyze your graphics.

Each conversion tool (Image Compressor, Image Resizer, Image Crop, Converters, and Image to PDF) processes your photo coordinates 100% locally in your web browser utilizing sandboxed memory threads. Your source pixels, colors, and layout configurations are never uploaded, serialized, or made available to our systems.

2. Advertising & Monitisation

To keep our browser suite free and unrestricted, we partner with advertising networks, including **Google AdSense** and **Adsterra**, to serve responsive graphic banner ads is localized placements.

These networks use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, and web beacons to tailor ad delivery. Third-party ad servers automatically receive your IP address, browser user-agent, and country metadata during requests to serve high quality, contextual, and safe banner ads.

Toolifya has no access to or control over cookie files managed by third-party providers. You can disable, revoke, or edit cookie tracking within your browser's system settings.

3. CCPA and GDPR Compliance Rights

Because we never ingest, transmit, parse, or store user information or graphics, our data operations are naturally compliant with regulatory frameworks such as California Community Privacy Rights (CCPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

With Toolifya, your data stays on your machine, preventing leaks, security vulnerabilities, or profiling tracking.

4. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this notice or would like clarification on our local technologies, please submit queries through our standard form on the About Contact Page.
