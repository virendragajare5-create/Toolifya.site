Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the layout order of PDF pages?

As soon as you upload multiple graphics, a sorted timeline window is created. You can use the '▲ Move Left' / '▼ Move Right' positional controls on each card to sort, reposition, or delete individual elements directly in the line stack.

What are Portrait and Landscape orientations used for?

Portrait formats orient the A4 or US Letter sheets as vertical documents—best for text document reports. Landscape designs shift sheets sideways (horizontal panels), which is ideal for photographic collections, slides, or high-definition portfolios.
