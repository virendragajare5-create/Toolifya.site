Frequently Asked Questions

Slicing graphics queries.

Will cropping compress the graphic quality?
No. Slicing only changes the width/height coordinates without running standard destructive artifacts over the visual frame.

What standard preset bounds are provided?
We contain instant rules: 1:1, 16:9, 4:3, 3:2, and 9:16. Free form parameters allow dragging border grids completely unconstrained.

Are transparent buffers supported?
Yes, setting WebP or PNG as your crop export destination targets returns flawless transparencies back, whereas JPEG overlays white backups.
