Frequently Asked Questions

Does cropping an image reduce its final resolution?

Yes, cropping maps out a subset of the original pixel coordinate block, reducing dimensions proportional to the crop box. However, we preserve the original pixels and never upscale artificially, keeping your final image crisp and unblurred.

What are the predefined aspect ratio selections?

We support preset standard layouts: 1:1, 16:9, 4:3, 3:2, 2:3, and 9:16. Selecting a preset locks the crop box proportions. Dragging any handle adapts the opposite side automatically. Free Form allows unrestricted adjustments.

Are my cropped images sent to remote databases?

Never. Toolifya runs entirely offline. All cropping operations, adjustments, and exports are processed inside your device's memory using secure Javascript canvas APIs.
