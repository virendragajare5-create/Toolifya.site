What happens to transparent outlines in PNG?
JPEGs do not carry alpha opacity lines. Transparent sections will draw out with whichever custom solid replacement color you pick within the sliders.

Will this free conversion reduce storage spaces?
Yes, PNG assets hold full lossless information which takes up lots of memory. Compressing them down to standard custom JPEGs lowers storage requirements significantly.
